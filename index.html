Our Approach

A Different Way to Build Media Visibility and Project Momentum

Our team works as media career and business development consultants. Our way of working is highly non-standard — and so is our success metric. We focus on what we call "future famous" actors, writers, directors, DPs, producers, and service providers.

Script writing contests, bulk emails, poorly formatted materials, and blind submissions to strangers are rarely successful. Our unique PR and promotions approach generates decision-maker access, media attention, and career advancement — often in a remarkably short period of time.

See How We Work
01

Strategic Evaluation

Every engagement begins with an initial strategic evaluation of all issues and all materials related to meeting your stated objectives. We understand where you are, what you have, and where you need to go.

02

Identify the Right Path

We know who's looking for specific ideas, who's looking for fresh styles, who's looking for niche-specific materials — and who's looking for their next possible project. We identify the optimal visibility or development path for your work.

03

Build Targeted Recognition

Using our unique PR and promotions approach, we help raise name recognition and funding by connecting you with those who are in a position to option, buy, greenlight, or invest — not random contacts, but the right people at the right time.

04

Support Your Objective

Whether your goal is a script sale, funding round, agent relationship, or career breakthrough, we work alongside you to support your project, talent positioning, or funding objective through to completion.
